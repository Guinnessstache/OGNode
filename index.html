OriginTrail v1 TRAC Recovery Tool

If you ran an OriginTrail v1 data holding node and staked TRAC as collateral, this tool lets you check your balance and withdraw tokens from the original ProfileStorage contract — the same flow as the old node-profile.origintrail.io site. Works with MetaMask and Ledger hardware wallets.

Known limitations

  • Reserved stake (in active jobs) cannot be withdrawn until those jobs are finalised on-chain — for many abandoned nodes this may be permanently stuck
  • This tool only supports Ethereum mainnet (the original v1 ODN)
  • WalletConnect v1 is used for Ledger; v2 support may be needed for newer Ledger Live versions
  • Contract source is unverified on Etherscan — selectors were reverse-engineered from bytecode
  • No audit — use at your own risk. Always verify the transaction in your wallet before signing

How it works

  • Balance is read directly from ProfileStorage via public Ethereum RPC (4 fallback endpoints)
  • Withdrawal routes through your ERC725 identity contract via execute()
  • Two steps: startTokenWithdrawal → 5 min wait → withdrawTokens
  • If a previous withdrawal was started and never finished, the app detects it and skips to the collect step
  • No backend — fully client-side. No keys or data leave your browser
